Frequently Asked Questions
How long until I see results?
▼

Results vary by channel. Paid ads and social media typically show results within weeks. SEO takes longer, usually 3-6 months, as search engines need time to crawl and rank your content. Expect 30-60 days of optimization before seeing significant changes depending on your specific channels and competition.

How often will I get updates?
▼

You'll receive monthly performance reports via email with detailed analytics and insights. You can also request updates anytime. We recommend scheduling bi-weekly or monthly check-in calls to discuss strategy, performance, and next steps.

Do I need to create content myself?
▼

No, we handle all content creation. Our team develops, designs, and optimizes content for each channel. We just need your approval on messaging, brand voice, and any specific information you'd like included. You maintain complete control over what goes out in your name.

What if I want to change my plan?
▼

We believe in flexibility. There are no long-term contracts that lock you in. You can adjust your services, budget, or scope as your business needs change. Just let your account manager know, and we'll make the changes.

How do algorithms affect my marketing?
▼

Every platform uses algorithms to determine what content to show users. We specialize in understanding and working with these algorithms to amplify your message. From Facebook's engagement algorithm to Google's ranking factors to TikTok's "For You" page, we model what each algorithm rewards and optimize accordingly.

Is my data secure?
▼

Absolutely. We use Firebase with enterprise-grade security, encrypted data transmission, and secure authentication. Your data is never shared with third parties. We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy standards. Your business information and performance data are for your eyes and ours only.
